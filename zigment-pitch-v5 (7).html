CORE INSIGHT
The conversation is the primary data object.

Every other system treats the lead record as the center. Conversations are afterthoughts — notes, logs, attachments. Zigment inverts this.

Traditional

Lead record at the center. Conversations are artifacts. Context is scattered across disconnected tools.

Zigment

Conversation at the center. Intent, sentiment, actions, revenue — all derived from it. Context is the system.

Conversation Graph is the system of record for the conversational world.

DEFENSIBILITY
Why this is hard to replicate.
vs. Incumbents — Salesforce, HubSpot

This is a foundational change — not an incremental product update. Changing the primary data object requires rethinking core technology, business model, and organizational structure.

vs. LLM Providers

Too close to outcome ownership. Customers need a platform that works for them — not one that competes for the relationship and the data.

vs. New Entrants

This requires working with live customer data in real business environments. It cannot be built as an idea-led, demo-stage product.

We aim to maintain a market lead of 6–12 months, constantly.
